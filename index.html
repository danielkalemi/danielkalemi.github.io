About me

Hello and welcome to my digital home!

My name is Daniel and I am a graduate student at UC Berkeley üêª. I'm currently pursuing a Master's in Development Engineering, with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics for Social Impact.

Tech is practical and fast. When I first flew to Greece for college, a young immigrant couple sitting next to me on the plane shared their struggle to grow their pastry shop. As a CS freshman, I offered to build their website for free. After a few days of coding, my first app was born and their problem was addressed! I love making a positive impact (& free muffins!!) and in the world of tech, there is so much potential to do so.

Fast-forward to today, I enjoy exploring new problem spaces through the power of data and product design, coming up with creative human-centered solutions that enhance the experience of the user. In my spare time, I love playing volleyball, hiking, and developing cross-platform apps!

My skills

I'm a Product Manger, Data Scientist and Software Engineer.

As an aspiring product manager and data scientist, I have dedicated my time and technical skills on a number of impactful projects, that among many, aim to improve access to the diagnosis of Diabetic Retinopathy (health-tech) or build disaster response detection systems, through Computer Vision applications, as well as other exciting initiatives on Universal Internet Access and Interplanetary Networking infrastructure.
